Research & Projects

Deep dive into laboratory projects, academic fellowships, and technology transfer milestones.

Post-Doctoral Researcher @ UNIST

Aug 2025 - Feb 2026

National project focused on engineering Pseudomonas denitrificans for efficient biosynthesis of Poly(3-hydroxypropionate) from glycerol and gluconate.

  • Engineered metabolic pathways in P. denitrificans for high-titer P(3HP) production.
  • Optimized fermentation conditions using glycerol and gluconate as renewable carbon sources.
  • Contributed to sustainable biopolymer production and carbon-neutral processing.

Research Fellow @ National Taiwan University

Nov 2024 - Apr 2025

Valorization of agricultural waste into premium structural Bacterial Cellulose (BC) for eco-friendly packaging.

  • Developed robust biological and chemical pretreatment models to convert peanut shell waste into a nutrient-rich media.
  • Modified cellulose nanofibrils through enzymatic and chemical cross-linking.
  • Achieved ~40% improvement in structural tensile strength and exceptional thermal barriers.
  • Awarded the NSTC Taiwan Postdoctoral Scholarship for bioreactor cultivation optimization.
  • Delivered ~15 hours of lectures on biomaterials, modifications, and applications during research fellowship at NTU.

Teaching Assistant @ University of Ulsan

Mar 2021 - Feb 2023
  • Two years as a Teaching Assistant during PhD (Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Molecular Biology, Genetics).
